Traverse City is a fairy-tale destination for a wedding -- no matter what the season! Spring brides may opt for a romantic ceremony among the millions of cherry blossoms that flower along the gently rolling hills of Old Mission and Leelanau counties. Or choose a vineyard setting on one of our two world-class wine trails. Summer is, of course, the perfect time for a one-of-a-kind beach wedding. Ditch the dress shoes for a comfy pair of flip-flops -- or just go barefoot in the sugar sand beach! And you won't find a more beautiful backdrop for your wedding photos than the sapphire blue and deep turquoise waters of Grand Traverse Bay. Fall brings a riot of yellows, crimson reds, and bright oranges to the hills and valleys in and around Traverse City. Couples might choose a rustic setting in the woods, or enjoy the panoramic views at one of our full-service resorts.
